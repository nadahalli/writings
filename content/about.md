I write about Bitcoin, cryptography, and trail running.

Professionally, I work on privacy at [Chainlink](https://chain.link/). Going back in time in reverse chronological order, I did some investment research at [L1D](https://l1d.com). Before my foray into venture capital, I [was](https://disco.ethz.ch/members/tejaswin) a Ph.D student at [ETH-Zürich](https://ethz.ch/en.html), working on [Bitcoin](https://tejaswin.com/bitcoin.pdf) and friends. Even before that, I was an engineer at Google Zürich, working on anti-spam, anti-hijacking, and automated web-browsing. Even earlier, I co-founded/worked-in anti-spam/analytics/web-search startups in Palo Alto, New York City, and Bangalore. Eons ago, I worked at Yahoo! and ThoughtWorks (both are still around!)
